Jorge A. Vélez

Short-CV:

I am an Economist and am currently a doctoral candidate at Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona (UAB) where I am due to complete my Ph.D. in Economics and Organizations by April 2019.

My research area focuses on the evaluation of public policies aimed to address market failures in the generation of knowledge. Within this area I have been working on two interrelated research streams:

  • The evaluation of public policies particularly those regarding Innovation and R&D.
  • Industrial Analysis and implication of R&D and Innovation.

I will be attending job market meetings in Naples, Italy 6-7 dec, 2018 and Madrid, Spain 13-15 dec, 2018.
